Ksenija is Kings Crest Global’s Chief Financial Officer.

Analytical, Precise, Engaging

• Operations and Finance management background
• 10+ years experience with remote work
• Finance and analytical expertise
With an analytical approach and excellent long-term vision, she applies a precise strategy to the finance department, with a focus on future growth for KCG and our clients. She also manages all aspects of client contracts and simultaneously liaises with project managers to forecast requirements and ensure plans run effectively.
