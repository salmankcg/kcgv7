RHR International Executive Bench

YEAR
2020
CLIENT
Executive Bench
SCOPE
UX Strategy, Visual Design, Interactive Design, Front-end Development, Content Management System, Shopify Integration
Executive Bench is a leadership coaching solution developed by RHR International. The Executive Bench team works with senior leaders in companies around the world, helping them to develop their skills and take on roles of higher complexity than ever before.
The Story
The Executive Bench team were in the process of developing highly progressive products, leveraging data to make their leadership consulting even more powerful. However, they did not have a suite of marketing materials to accurately demonstrate the innovative nature of their work.
Where Kings Crest Global Comes In
We have developed a long-term relationship with RHR International. We continuously help them to create compelling, exciting and fresh marketing assets. Despite the challenges of the pandemic, we have collaborated flexibly with the RHR team to develop websites, landing pages, SEO campaigns, PPC campaigns and more.
A Brand New Look and Feel
Assessing the Executive Bench service, we took a risk and developed an entirely different brand approach - one that was innovative and energetic.

Their new website and sales assets continuously receive positive reinforcement from the market. Here’s a sample of what we made:

• 1x lead-focused website
• 10x sales toolkits
• 5x practice overview presentations
• 7x email campaigns
• 1x survey portal and materials
Increased Web Traffic
Conversions on the Executive Bench website are at an all-time high. The inbound calls that the team have received are qualified leads and the response has been positive.
