Saul Tessler is the founder and CEO of Kings Crest Global. His mission is to ensure that we solve our client’s problems in unique and unexpected ways, continuously helping them to make the world a better place. He is motivated by crafting a company culture that centers on happiness; a diverse team of talented people, delivering meaningful work with passion and joy. In addition to his work as CEO, Saul applies his expertise to help clients solve startup and scaling problems, giving them the confidence (and the precious time!) to focus on what makes them unique.

“I don’t know what I want to be when I grow up” is a phrase that has defined Saul’s professional journey; a ceaseless curiosity that has inspired him to confidently create new business ventures time and time again. With a master’s in Conducting from Juilliard and multimedia training from Harvard, his first forward-thinking business venture was to combine the world of digital with music, creating ways to transcribe musical scores onto computers. Captivated by the potential of the online space, he then launched one of the first online portals for students to find and rent student properties. This was his introduction to the world of startups, which took him across the startup scene. He then developed his corporate expertise and worked on Wall Street for one of the second largest funds in the United States, before moving to Barcelona to teach entrepreneurship at the college level.

Connecting with such a wide range of brilliant international thinkers while working as a professor sparked Saul’s idea for Kings Crest Global, and so he began building the company’s international team of experts. Saul’s varied, textured and unique life enables him to deliver truly unique perspectives on business, and that is only further supported by his empathetic approach. Saul can always be found exploring new ideas, new countries and new concepts, regularly taking the road less travelled to get there, and picking up unique experiences along the way.
